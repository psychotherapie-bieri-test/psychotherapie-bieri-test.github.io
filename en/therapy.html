Therapy

Illnesses, crises and stress can drag us down and make everyday life a struggle. Therapy can help.

Exploring with a therapist what is troubling you, what is important to you, and what gets in your way can be a great relief. It can open up new ways of coping, and set the stage for working together towards the changes you are hoping for.

I like to work with empirically validated methods such as cognitive-behavioral therapy, acceptance and commitment therapy, schema therapy, and mindfulness practices.

My areas of expertise include:

  • Culture shock and cultural adjustment
  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Addictions
  • Chronic pain
  • Loneliness
  • Grief
  • Shyness
  • Burnout
  • Relationship problems
  • Chronic mental illness
